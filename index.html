Methodology

Built on rigorous science.

Every batch synthesized at Stratus Biolabs undergoes a multi-stage quality verification pipeline before it ever reaches a researcher's bench.

No shortcuts, no marketing chemistry — every lot ships with a third-party verified Certificate of Analysis.

01

Solid-Phase Synthesis

Fmoc SPPS methodology with real-time coupling monitoring to ensure sequence fidelity at every residue.

02

HPLC Purification

Preparative reverse-phase HPLC targeting ≥99% purity, verified by analytical HPLC on every batch.

03

Mass Spectrometry

Each lot confirmed by ESI-MS or MALDI-TOF to verify molecular weight and structural integrity.

04

CoA & Lyophilization

Full Certificate of Analysis with every order. Shipped as lyophilized powder for maximum stability.
