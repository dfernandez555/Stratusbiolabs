Refund Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

All sales are final. Because our products are research chemicals and lyophilized peptides whose integrity depends on controlled handling and storage once they leave our custody, we do not accept returns or issue refunds for any product after delivery — except in the limited circumstances described below (damage in transit, defective product, or non-delivery).

1. No Returns or Refunds for Delivered Products

Once a product has been delivered, we cannot verify how it was stored or handled. Storage temperature, light exposure, humidity, and time-since-opening all materially affect the integrity of research peptides and reagents. For this reason we cannot resell returned products, and we cannot refund them.

We do not accept returns or refunds for:

  • Change of mind, ordering by mistake, or no-longer-needed orders
  • Opened, unsealed, or partially used products
  • Products stored outside of the recommended conditions after delivery
  • Failed experiments, unsuccessful research outcomes, or results that did not match your expectations (our products are sold for research only and we make no efficacy claims)
  • Products refused at delivery or returned to us by the carrier due to an incorrect address you provided
  • Products held, seized, or destroyed by customs or a regulatory authority in your destination country

Please carefully review your cart, shipping address, and product selection before placing an order.

2. Limited Exceptions

The following are the only circumstances in which we will issue a refund or replacement. Each has a strict time window and documentation requirement.

2.1  Damaged in transit

If your shipment arrives with visible physical damage to the outer packaging or to the product vials inside (e.g. broken vials, leaking containers, crushed boxes), you may request a replacement or refund.

Time window: Notify us within 48 hours of delivery.

Documentation required:

  • Photographs of the outer packaging
  • Photographs of each damaged item
  • Your order ID

Resolution: At our discretion, we will issue a free replacement of the damaged item(s) or a refund to your original payment method. We may also file a carrier claim and ask you to retain the damaged items for inspection.

2.2  Defective product (fails analytical specification)

If a product you receive demonstrably fails the analytical specifications stated on its accompanying Certificate of Analysis (CoA) — for example, the purity or identity is materially below what was reported — you may request a replacement or refund.

Time window: Notify us within 14 days of delivery.

Documentation required:

  • Independent analytical testing showing the discrepancy (e.g. third-party HPLC or MS report)
  • The original CoA shipped with your product, for comparison
  • Your order ID and product lot number

Resolution: At our discretion, we will issue a free replacement of the affected lot or a refund to your original payment method. Internal failed-experiment results, anecdotal observations, or non-quantitative claims do not qualify as documentation of a defect.

2.3  Order never delivered

If carrier tracking indicates your order was not delivered after a reasonable period (15 business days for domestic shipments, 30 business days for international), contact us so we can investigate with the carrier.

Time window: Notify us within 30 days of the original shipment date.

Resolution: We will investigate with the carrier. If the carrier confirms the package is lost in transit (not merely delayed), we will issue a free replacement or refund at our discretion. If tracking shows the package was delivered but you did not receive it, we will work with the carrier on a claim — resolution depends on the carrier's findings.

3. How Refunds Are Issued

When we approve a refund:

  • Card payments (via MoonPay or other on-ramp): refund is issued in the cryptocurrency settlement amount we received, sent to the wallet of your choice. We cannot reverse the original card transaction because MoonPay acts as the merchant of record and we receive only the cryptocurrency settlement.
  • Cryptocurrency (direct, via NOWPayments or similar): refund is issued in the same cryptocurrency at the equivalent USD value at the time of refund, sent to the wallet you provide.
  • Cash App or Zelle: refund is issued by Cash App or Zelle to the account that originally paid us.

Refunds are typically issued within 7 business days of approval. Network confirmation times for cryptocurrency refunds depend on the chosen chain.

4. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you have a concern about your order, please contact us first so we have an opportunity to resolve it. Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute before contacting us, or for an order outside the limited refund exceptions above, may result in the suspension of your account and forfeiture of any in-progress orders. We may also pursue collection of disputed amounts plus any fees we incur.

5. How to Request a Refund

Email info@stratusbiolabs.com with:

  • Subject line: “Refund Request — [Your Order ID]”
  • Your full order ID (format: SB-XXXXXX)
  • The product(s) affected
  • The reason for the request (one of the exceptions in Section 2)
  • The required documentation listed in the applicable section

We will respond within 2 business days with a decision or a request for additional information.

6. Order Cancellations

If you placed an order but the order has not yet been dispatched to fulfillment, you may request cancellation by emailing info@stratusbiolabs.com with your order ID. Once an order is in the fulfillment process (typically within hours of payment confirmation), it cannot be cancelled.

If you paid for an order that has not yet been dispatched and you request cancellation in time, we will refund the full amount via the original payment method.
