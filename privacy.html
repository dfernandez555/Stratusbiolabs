Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Stratus Biolabs (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you visit stratusbiolabs.com or place an order through our website. We are a U.S.-based research-chemical supplier; all products are sold strictly for research use only.

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, contact us at info@stratusbiolabs.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you place an order, contact us, or apply for our affiliate program, we collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Shipping address (street, city, state, ZIP, country)
  • Institution or company name (if provided)
  • Research field (selected from a dropdown at checkout)
  • Order contents and order history
  • Affiliate-program application details (handle, platform, audience size, notes)
  • Any free-form notes you provide via our contact form or checkout notes field

Information collected automatically

  • IP address and basic device information (browser, operating system)
  • Pages visited on our site and approximate timing
  • A research-use-attestation cookie set when you confirm research-use intent on entry
  • A cart-state value stored in your browser's localStorage so your cart persists between visits

Information we do NOT collect or store

  • We do not store your full payment card details. Payment information is handled directly by our payment processors (see below). Their forms — not ours — collect your card data.
  • We do not run third-party advertising trackers, marketing cookies, or analytics pixels for ad targeting.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate with you about your order status, shipping, and customer support
  • Verify research-use eligibility and process compliance attestations
  • Pay affiliate commissions where applicable
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized use of our site
  • Comply with applicable law and respond to lawful requests from authorities
  • Improve our website, products, and customer experience

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use a small number of cookies and browser-local-storage values, all strictly functional:

  • Research-use attestation token — set when you confirm research-use intent on entry, so you don't see the entry prompt on every visit
  • Cart contents — stored in localStorage so your cart survives between visits
  • Cloudflare security cookies — set by our hosting provider for bot detection and rate limiting

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or cross-site tracking.

6. Data Retention

We retain order records and customer communications for as long as needed to fulfill the order, support warranty/refund requests, and meet legal record-keeping obligations (typically 7 years for tax and accounting purposes). You may request earlier deletion subject to those legal requirements (see Section 8).

7. Data Security

We protect your information using industry-standard safeguards: HTTPS encryption for all site traffic; encrypted storage of order records on Cloudflare's infrastructure; server-side validation of all order data; rate limiting; and restricted administrator access protected by a password authenticated header. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your information (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Restriction or objection — ask us to limit or stop certain uses of your information
  • Portability — receive your information in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent — where we rely on your consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@stratusbiolabs.com with the subject line “Privacy Request.” We will respond within 30 days.

California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what categories of personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of any “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

European Economic Area / United Kingdom residents (GDPR)

If you reside in the EEA or UK, our legal bases for processing your information are: (a) performance of the contract when you place an order; (b) compliance with legal obligations; and (c) our legitimate interests in operating and securing the website. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Children

Our website and products are intended for use by qualified researchers and adults aged 21 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International Users

Our website is operated from the United States. If you access our site from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S. By using the site you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Material changes will be announced through a notice on the website. Your continued use of the site after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
